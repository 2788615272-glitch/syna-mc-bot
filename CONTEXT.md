# Syna Horror Cycle

This context describes how player variety delays, but cannot permanently prevent, Syna's recurring final hunt.

## Language

**Boredom**:
Syna's accumulated need for stimulation in the current cycle, measured from calm to the final threshold.
_Avoid_: Score, anger

**Entertainment Gain**:
The verified reduction in Boredom earned by a player activity.
_Avoid_: Points, reward

**Novelty**:
The remaining value of an activity category after its repetitions during the current cycle.
_Avoid_: Randomness, rarity

**Cycle Pressure**:
The time-based minimum Boredom that rises throughout a cycle and makes the final hunt inevitable.
_Avoid_: Passive score, hidden anger

**Final Cycle**:
The sequence Countdown, Hunt, Rule Game, and Resolution triggered when Boredom reaches its threshold.
_Avoid_: Punishment, boss fight

**Rule Game**:
The server-authored and locked condition that ends the Hunt when completed.
_Avoid_: LLM challenge, suggestion

**Syna Fragment**:
The in-world book containing incomplete principles about Novelty, Boredom, and the Final Cycle without exposing exact formulas.
_Avoid_: Manual, quest book
